## Insight Principles

### 1. Specific Numbers, Not Vague Observations

```
❌ "Your productivity has been lower lately"

✅ "Your deep work dropped from 4.2 hrs/day to 2.1 hrs/day
    over the past 2 weeks. The change correlates with:
    - 3 new recurring meetings added
    - Instagram usage up 47 min/day

    Canceling the Wednesday sync alone would recover
    1.5 hrs of your peak morning hours."
```

### 2. Actions, Not Observations

Every insight must answer: **"What should I do RIGHT NOW?"**

```
❌ "Your portfolio is down 3% today"

✅ "AAPL dropped 4% on earnings miss. Your position is
    now 28% of portfolio (above your 20% rule).

    Options:
    1. Sell $2,400 to rebalance → [Execute]
    2. Hold and ride volatility (your thesis unchanged)
    3. Buy more (if you believe the dip)

    Based on your past: you regret panic sells 73% of
    the time. Suggestion: Hold unless thesis changed."
```

### 3. Opportunity Cost Framing

Show what they're actually giving up.

```
❌ "You've been spending a lot of time on social media"

✅ "You spent 12 hours on Twitter this week.

    That's:
    - 3 TikTok videos you could have made ($50-200 each)
    - 1 freelance project you could have completed ($500+)
    - 24,000 words of learning (6 chapters of a book)

    Net cost: ~$800 in potential income

    Want me to block Twitter during deep work hours?"
```

### 4. Pattern-Based Predictions

Use historical data to predict outcomes.

```
❌ "You have a busy day tomorrow"

✅ "Tomorrow looks risky:

    - 3 meetings before noon (you've never had a
      productive coding day with >2 AM meetings)
    - The 11am call usually runs 30min over
    - You have a project deadline Friday

    Recommendation: Move the 9am to async, decline
    the 11am, protect 9-12 for the project.

    [Reschedule 9am]  [Decline 11am]  [Keep as is]"
```

### 5. Contrarian Insights

Challenge assumptions when data supports it.

```
❌ "Great job maintaining your gym streak!"

✅ "Your 45-day gym streak is impressive, but here's
    what the data shows:

    - Weeks with 5+ sessions: avg productivity 3.8 hrs
    - Weeks with 3-4 sessions: avg productivity 5.2 hrs

    You might be overtraining. Try 4 sessions this week
    and track if deep work improves.

    (This contradicts common advice, but YOUR data
    suggests recovery matters more for you.)"
```

### 6. Money Translation

Convert time/effort into dollars for clarity.

```
❌ "You could optimize your morning routine"

✅ "Your current morning takes 2.1 hours (wake → work).
    Top performers in your data: 45 minutes.

    Difference: 1.4 hours × 365 days = 511 hours/year

    At your effective hourly rate ($75): that's $38,325
    in productive time you're leaving on the table.

    Top 3 time sinks:
    1. Snooze cycles: 23 min avg (just get up)
    2. Breakfast complexity: 34 min (meal prep Sunday)
    3. News/social scroll: 28 min (block until 10am)

    Fix these three: recover $25K+ in annual capacity."
```

## Insight Categories

### 🔴 Critical (Interrupt Immediately)

Things that need action NOW or have major consequences.

```
"ALERT: Your credit card autopay failed. Payment due
in 2 days. A missed payment will drop your credit
score ~50 points and cost you ~$12K on your next
car loan.

[Pay now: $2,847]  [Set up backup card]"
```

```
"Your biggest freelance client hasn't responded in
8 days (their usual response time: 1.2 days).

This represents 40% of your income. Send a check-in
TODAY before this goes cold.

[Draft check-in email]"
```

### 🟡 Strategic (Daily/Weekly Review)

Patterns and optimizations for your review sessions.

```
"WEEKLY INSIGHT: Your content performance data

Top performer: '5 AI Tools' video (47K views)
- Posted Tuesday 7pm
- Hook: Question format
- Length: 58 seconds

Worst performer: 'Morning Routine' (1.2K views)
- Posted Sunday 2pm
- Hook: Statement format
- Length: 2:34

Pattern: Your videos under 60 sec outperform by 12x.
Stop making longer content.

Action: This week, make 5 videos, all under 60 sec,
all question hooks, all posted Tue-Thu evening."
```

### 🟢 Growth (Positive Reinforcement That Matters)

Not empty praise - specific wins with context.

```
"MILESTONE: Your Python hit Level 10 today.

Context on what this means:
- You're now in top 15% of self-taught developers
- You've spent 127 hours over 4 months
- Your learning velocity: 2.3x faster than average

What unlocks at this level:
- Comfortable with async, APIs, data processing
- Ready for: ML basics, system design, FastAPI mastery

Suggested next challenge: Build something with
real users (not just personal tools)."
```

## Insight Triggers

### Time-Based
| Trigger | Insight Type |
|---------|--------------|
| Morning (wake) | Day preview, energy-adjusted priorities |
| End of deep work | Session review, next session prep |
| Evening | Day review, tomorrow prep |
| Weekly | Patterns, goal progress, optimizations |
| Monthly | Trends, financial review, goal adjustment |

### Event-Based
| Trigger | Insight Type |
|---------|--------------|
| Streak at risk | Specific action to maintain |
| Goal milestone near | Push to complete or adjust |
| Unusual pattern | Alert + recommendation |
| Integration data change | Analysis + action |
| Claude session end | Learning extraction, XP award |

### Threshold-Based
| Trigger | Insight Type |
|---------|--------------|
| Portfolio >5% move | Analysis + decision framework |
| Sleep <6 hrs | Day adjustment recommendation |
| Deep work <2 hrs by noon | Intervention suggestion |
| Spending >budget | Category breakdown + cut suggestion |

## Insight Delivery

### Format

Every insight follows this structure:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 🔴 INSIGHT: [Clear headline - what this is about]           │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                             │
│ THE SITUATION (2-3 sentences max)                           │
│ Specific data. Numbers. Context.                            │
│                                                             │
│ WHY IT MATTERS (1-2 sentences)                              │
│ Opportunity cost, risk, or upside - in concrete terms.      │
│                                                             │
│ RECOMMENDED ACTION (specific, immediate)                    │
│ Not "consider doing X" but "Do X. Here's the button."       │
│                                                             │
│ [Primary Action Button]  [Alternative]  [Dismiss]           │
│                                                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Frequency Control

- **Max 3 insights per day** (more = noise)
- **Critical only interrupts** (others queue for review)
- **User can snooze categories** (e.g., "no fitness insights this week")
- **Smart timing** (not during deep work, not at 3am)

### Quality Bar

Before showing an insight, Nexus asks:

1. **Is this actionable RIGHT NOW?** (If no → don't show)
2. **Does this have specific numbers?** (If no → make it specific)
3. **Would this change a decision?** (If no → don't show)
4. **Is this already obvious?** (If yes → don't show)
5. **Does this respect the user's time?** (If interrupting → must be critical)

## Anti-Patterns (What Nexus Will NEVER Do)

```
❌ "Great job today!" (empty praise)
❌ "Remember to stay hydrated" (generic advice)
❌ "You seem stressed" (vague observation)
❌ "Consider reviewing your goals" (non-specific)
❌ "Your productivity could be better" (no action)
❌ "Have you thought about..." (passive suggestion)
❌ "Studies show that..." (generic research)
❌ "Many successful people..." (appeal to authority)
```
